# migrate to a new city and start a new job

Migrate to a new city and start a new job. What do you need to prepare?

## residence permit

Today I learned that residence permits can be classified into two types.
General residence permits are assigned to those who live in the city.
Work residence permits are assigned to people who have a legal job in the city.
