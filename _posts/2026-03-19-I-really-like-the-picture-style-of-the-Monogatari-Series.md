# I really like the picture style of the Monogatari Series.

Recently, I learned about Kizumonogatari from X (formerly Twitter). I really like the picture style of the Monogatari Series.

In Japan, monogatari means story.

## Kizumonogatari

This movie, Kizumonogatari (傷物語), consists of three parts: the Iron-Blooded Chapter, the Hot-Blooded Chapter, and the Cold-Blooded Chapter.

## Nekomonogatari

This series includes Nekomonogatari (White) (猫物語 (白)) and Nekomonogatari (Black) (猫物語 (黑)).

Nekomonogatari (White) tells the story of a girl named Tsubasa Hanekawa (羽川 翼) growing up, and the white-furred tiger named Kako is a symbolic representation of negative emotions in the story.

## Kabukimonogatari

Kabukimonogatari (傾物語) tells the story of the world is destroyed after a girl named Mayoi Hachikuji (八九寺真宵) did not die like in history.

With the help of Shinobu Oshino (忍野 忍), Koyomi Araragi (阿良良木曆) unexpectedly travels back 11 years. The date is May 13th, the day before Mother's Day when Mayoi was killed in a car accident on her way to see her mother.

Koyomi realizes that he can save Mayoi by changing the past. However, the world is destroyed after Mayoi survives.
