# National Games free scenic area tickets

The 15th National Games will be held in Guangdong, Hong Kong and Macao from 9 to 21 November 2025.
Guangzhou Tourism Bureau distributes 756,000 National Games free scenic area tickets to all residents and tourists, including Guangzhou Zoo, Baiyun Mountain, Yunluo Botanical Garden, and Yunxi Botanical Garden.
